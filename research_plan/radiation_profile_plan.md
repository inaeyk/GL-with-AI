# Radiation Profile Plan

This is a placeholder for the future gravitational-wave/radiation project. A full plan should be developed only after the black-string reproduction diagnostics are robust and convergent.

Eventual observables:

- Waveform.
- Spectrum.
- Radiated energy by mode.
- Angular distribution.
- Dependence on GL generation times and Fourier modes.

Inputs expected from the reproduction phase:

- Reliable apparent-horizon diagnostics.
- Tracked generation times.
- Fourier-mode content of unstable string segments.
- Convergence estimates for the relevant source dynamics.
